Who is The Notorious One?

His true identity shall remain mystery forever

He comes from a land far far away, a little place that some people call The Sanctuary. He was born into this consumer age, he saw the world around him prosper but also stumble and fail. Life never went easy on him but he stood his ground. Life was not always fair to him, yet he continues to another round. This guy he used to have a dream, that one day this world will change, but as time passed and the world seemed not to be changing he decided to take the steps himself, he was determined to sacrafice anyhting to make this world a better place. It was fun till it lasted, now it's his last time to stare at the dawn of the sun. And that was the story of The Notorious One.

Here he is, making the world a better place.
